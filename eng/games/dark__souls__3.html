Dark Souls III

  Dark Souls III is an Action/RPG computer game developed by FromSoftware for Windows, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One platforms. The game was announced on June 15, 2015, at the Microsoft conference during the E3 2015 exhibition. It was released on March 24, 2016, in Japan for Xbox One and PS4. The worldwide release on consoles and PC took place on April 12, 2016.
