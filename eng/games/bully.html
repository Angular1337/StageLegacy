Bully

  Bully, also known as Canis Canem Edit, is a third—person, open-world action-adventure video game developed by Rockstar Vancouver and released by Rockstar Games in October 2006 for the PlayStation 2 video game console. In total, in addition to the original release, there are several re-releases of the game. In 2008, the game was released for Xbox 360, Wii and PC under the title Bully: Scholarship Edition. In this edition, the game has been significantly redesigned — moved to a new engine, better models and textures are used; new story tasks and lessons have been added. In 2012, the original 2006 version was re-released on PlayStation 3 without changes in the PlayStation 2 Classic series; in 2016, it was also re-released on PlayStation 4 (with the addition of a trophy system and a higher screen resolution). In 2016, Bully: Anniversary Edition was released on mobile devices running iOS and Android systems. This option is similar to the Scholarship Edition, but adapted for portable devices. The porting work was done by Grove Street Games. The game tells the story of teenager Jimmy Hopkins, an avid bully who has changed schools before. Jimmy's new school year begins at a boarding school with a very bad reputation in the small American town of Bulworth.
