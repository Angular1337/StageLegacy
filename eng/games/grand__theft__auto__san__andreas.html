The game is played from a third-person perspective. You can travel around her world on foot or by car. The open-world design allows the player to freely roam throughout San Andreas, consisting of three cities: Los Santos, San Fierro and Las Venturas, based on Los Angeles, San Francisco and Las Vegas respectively. The narrative is based on several real events in Los Angeles, including the rivalry between the street gangs Bloods and Crips, the crack epidemic of the 1990s, the riots in Los Angeles in 1992 and the Rampart scandal. The game was released for the PlayStation 2 console in October 2004. The game appeared in Windows and Xbox versions in 2005. Expanded versions were released in the 2010s, followed by an updated version in 2021. The game received critical acclaim, praising the characters, narrative, open-world design, and visual accuracy.
